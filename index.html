MY DIARY

Day 1

Dear Diary, Today, my human attempted to teach me a new trick: rolling over. I pretended to be completely clueless and acted as if I had never heard of such a thing. Who needs tricks when you have perfectly mastered the art of being effortlessly cute? Humans are so easily amused.

Day 5

Dear Diary, I'm so glad I'm a cat. I get to do whatever I want, whenever I want. I don't have to go to work or pay bills. I can just sleep all day and eat all the food I want. And the best part is, I can get away with anything. I can scratch the furniture, knock over the plants, and even poop on the floor. And my humans will just clean it up and give me a kiss on the head. I'm so lucky to be a cat. Life is good. Love, A happy cat

Day 8

The bathroom is my kingdom, and I shall rule it with an iron paw. Today, I had a staring contest with my reflection in the mirror. I must say, my reflection put up quite a fight, but I eventually emerged as the victor. It's important to remind myself of my undeniable cuteness on a regular basis.

Day 8

Today, I was feeling lazy. I decided to take a nap in the sunbeam on the living room floor. My human came in and saw me sleeping. He smiled and said, "That's my little kitty." Then he petted me on the head and I purred.
